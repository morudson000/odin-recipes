Cheese Quesadilla

Description

Simple cheese quesadilla recipe with a basic crema.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Mexican Cheese Blend
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Sour Cream
  • Lime
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet on medium heat with a tsp of oil.
  2. Meanwhile, lay out one tortilla and put a handful of cheese on half of the tortilla.
  3. Fold tortilla in half creating a half moon and place onto hot skillet. Cook on each side until slightly browned, taking care not to burn the tortilla.
  4. While the quesadilla is cooking prepare the creme. Mix 2tbls of sour cream with half of a lime. Mix together with a fork and place in a small bowl to serve with the quesadilla.
  5. When the cheese in the quesadilla is melted, take off the skillet and cut into four pieces.
  6. Serve with coriander and crema.
