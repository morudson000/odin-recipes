Easy Gnocchi Recipe

Description

This recipe teaches you how to make potato gnocci that tastes much better than store bought gnocchi. These are also freezer-friendly.

You can sautee this with butter and bacon, pesto, or basic marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Russet potatos
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash potatos and bake them in the oven at 450F for 45-60 min.
  2. Peel potatos and cut each one into 4 slives. Use a potato ricer to press each piece over a clean work surface.
  3. Drizzle 1 beaten egg over the potatos and dot the top of the potatos with bits of ricotta cheese.
  4. Mix 1tps salt and 1tsp pepper into 1 cup flour then sift the flour mixture over the potatoes.
  5. Mix everything together and gently knead, roll and fold the dough. Knead until flour is incorporated and a soft and smooth dough has formed.
  6. Roll dough into a log and divide into 8 pieces. Roll each piece into thin strips and cut strips into 1-inch pieces. Roll over a gnocci paddle for a ridge pattern. Transfer to a parchment-lined and four-dusted baking sheet.
  7. Cook gnocci in 3-4 batches in salted water. When they start to float, cook for 1 minute then lift with a sieve to drain then transfer to warm sauce or finish in a skillet.
