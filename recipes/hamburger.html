Description

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ketchup
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Cheese
  • Brioche Buns
  • Pickles
  • Red Onion

Steps

  1. Prepare patties into 8 equal portions(3oz) each.
  2. Prepare toppings such as cutting the onions and cutting the cheese.
  3. Lightly butter both sides of the buns and toast on a griddle over medium heat until golden brown. Transfer to a rack.
  4. Increase skillet to medium-high heat and cook patties. Smash each into a thin patty. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic poweder. Flip and sear on the opposite side.
  5. Add cheese and top with a second patty. Remove from head and serve warm over toasted buns with toppings.
